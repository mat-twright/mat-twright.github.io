The Values of a Two-Time Champion

Sammy Wright

Bold

No champion secures victory without daring moves. Whether it’s splitting two boules with an incisive throw or aiming to knock down an audacious spare, a Bunny Champion takes and lands bold leaps.

Unparalleled Talent

Having never practiced petanque or skittles, most would not have taken to these sports so well. The magic of the cup brings the best out of all athletes. All competitors quickly master sports than have taken years for others, and also ones we have made up days before people arrive.

Nerves

More precisely, how to deal with them. Not many have the metal to hold up under such pressure. Whether it’s Grandad discovering his ice-cold placement on the croquet lawns or Simon (Dad) deciding which red to pair with the lamb, nerves are tested all throughout mighty tournament.

Noble

An unnoble champion is no true champion. Having your name immortalised is no mean feat, and no bunny champion should ever forget this.

Years

At the end of the day, the Bunny Champion does it for legacy. When the trophy is taller than its holder, a champion will still remember their victorious years along with the meals, the laughs and the competition that came with it.
